Last updated: 16 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the app "WHO CARES MORE?" handles user information.

## What data we collect

**None.**

WHO CARES MORE? does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- We do not use analytics services.
- We do not use crash reporting services.
- We do not use advertising networks.
- We do not use tracking technologies.
- We do not require an account or login.
- We do not access your contacts, location, microphone, or camera.

## Data stored on your device

WHO CARES MORE? stores the following information **locally on your device only**, using the operating system's standard preferences storage:

- Your saved analysis history (locally only)
- Scan and deep-report credit balances
- In-app purchase entitlement state (locally cached)
- Processed purchase identifiers (used to prevent double-granting credits)

This data never leaves your device. Deleting the app removes all of it.

## In-App Purchases

WHO CARES MORE? offers optional In-App Purchases processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not handle, store, or see your payment information.

For information about how Apple and Google handle your payment data, see:

- Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Sharing

The app provides a "Share" feature using the native iOS / Android share sheet. The content you share is controlled entirely by you and the recipient app you choose. We do not see, store, or process any shared content.

## Children

WHO CARES MORE? does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences; specific age suitability is reflected by the App Store / Play Store rating.

## Your rights under GDPR / DSGVO

Even though we do not collect personal data, under the EU General Data Protection Regulation you have the right to:

- Confirmation that we do not process your data (we do not).
- Erasure of any data (none exists on our side; deleting the app removes local data).
- Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after a change means you accept the revised policy.
