## Frequently asked questions

**Restore Purchases isn't bringing my entitlement back.**
Open the in-app Settings (or paywall) and tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you are signed in to the same App Store / Google Play account that originally made the purchase.

**How do I cancel a subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account. WHO CARES MORE? doesn't store any subscription information server-side.

**My credits or saved history disappeared.**
WHO CARES MORE? stores everything locally on your device. If you reinstall the app or clear app data, that local state is removed. Subscription and non-consumable purchases can be recovered via **Restore Purchases**; consumable purchases that were already used cannot be restored.
